About Tikip

Tikip is a free tool designed to help you retrieve public TikTok user profiles. With our TikTok profile lookup service, you can easily find user location, account details, and more.

FAQ

How does it work? Tikip fetches publicly available data using the username you provide. This includes their display name, bio, follower counts, and an estimated country or region.
